Paul's Homemade Pasta

Ingrediants

  • 2 Â½ cups of flour (preferably 00/semolina flour)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of sea salt

Directions:

1. Pour your flour onto a cleaned surface and use a small bowl or spoon to create a well in the middle, leaving flour on the bottom. Crack the eggs into the well and add the olive oil and salt. Begin to whisk the ingredients around in the flour, gradually pulling the outside flour into the eggs. As the dough begins to thicken up, you can start to use your hands to knead. Knead the dough for around 10 minutes or until the dough is smooth. You can add some extra flour if the dough is too sticky and some water if it's too dry. Form the dough into a ball and wrap with plastic wrap. Let sit at room temperature for at least 30 minutes.

2. Roll the pasta dough into your desired shape when ready, either by hand or with a pasta maker. You will need some more flour to coat your rolling surface.

3. Cook for about 2 minutes, serve, and enjoy.

Description

Homemade cooked pasta is a comforting and versatile culinary creation. Crafted from simple ingredients like flour and eggs, it offers a delightful combination of softness and texture when cooked to perfection. The fresh, tender noodles can be customized to suit various dishes, from classic spaghetti with tomato sauce to gourmet recipes featuring creamy Alfredo or rich Bolognese sauces. Homemade pasta often embodies a labor of love, with its preparation reflecting the care and creativity of the chef. Whether served as a family favorite or a gourmet masterpiece, homemade cooked pasta never fails to satisfy the senses and evoke a sense of warmth and nostalgia.
